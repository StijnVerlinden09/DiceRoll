Dice Points
  Each dice roll can give you a different
  amount of points.

  1 point:  1 Pair
  2 points: 2 Pair
  2 points: 3 of a Kind
  3 points: 4 of a Kind
  4 points: Full House     
            (3 of a kind and 2 of a kind)
  5 points: Small Straight
            (1-2-3-4 or 2-3-4-5)
  6 points: Large Straight
            (1-2-3-4-5)
  7 points: Yahtzee
            (Five of a kind)
